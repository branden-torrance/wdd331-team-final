Group 1 Space Pattern Library

Theme: Space

For our CSS Zen Garden, our team has decided to use the theme of “space”. This space theme will revolve around using design elements such as planets, moons, stars, rocket ship, and an astronaut. Our plan is to create these images using CSS shapes, and perhaps other methods as we research more about how to create our images. We plan to incorporate our theme into our design in multiple ways. Those ways include:

  • Having our header text in a centralized planet image in the center of the screen.
  • Having the separate sections, “The Road To Enlightenment”, “So What is This About”, “Participation”, “Benefits”, and “Requirements” contain their paragraph titles in separate “moons” located around the main planet. As the user hovers over each of these moons, the moon will expand and give you the full text of each section.
  • Having static star images in the background that will periodically “twinkle” while viewing the page. This twinkling effect will likely be achieved by gradually increasing the contrast of the star’s color, then changing the contrast back to its original level.
  • Having a rocket ship image travel around or across the screen.
  • Having an astronaut image occasionally drift across the screen, perhaps in random locations.

*Note: As we continue to develop this project, we are likely to change certain elements of our design as we research and discover ways of implementing our plan. Such things might include changing the way our images move/animate on screen, depending on the time/complexity of the task.
